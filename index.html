My Favorite Montreal Restaurants so far

Au Pain Doree

Cute french boulangerie specializing in various breads and pastries. Amazing croissants & almond croissants.

Sota BBQ Coreen

Delicious and cheap all you can eat Korean BBQ. $14.99 after 11p.m. on weekends.

Boulangerie Guillaume

Amazing and eccletic bakery. Delicious and inventive bread and pastry and a short walk from La Gare!
